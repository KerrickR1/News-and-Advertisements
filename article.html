Downtown Atlanta High-Rise Evacuated Over Gas Leak

Residents of a downtown Atlanta high-rise had to evacuate their building overnight as fire crews investigated a possible gas leak. Firefighters say the leak started in the boiler room of the Landmark Condominium complex on the 200 block of Piedmont Avenue. The building was evacuated as a precaution while crews worked at the scene. No injuries were reported connected to the leak, and residents are back inside their homes on Thursday morning.
